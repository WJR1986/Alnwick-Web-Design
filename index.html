About Us

Welcome to Alnwick Pet Care.

We offer dog walking and pet sitting services based in Alnwick, Northumberland. All our services are flexible, and we aim to provide a fully bespoke service.

Learn More

Services

Dog Walks

Flexible & Safe.

We only walk dogs on a one-to-one basis or dogs from the same household. We believe this is safer for the dogs and gives the owner peace of mind.

All walks are designed to be flexible in terms of location and duration, to make the experience focused on your dog.

Pet Sitting

Time away with complete peace of mind!

We provide pet-sitting services at your property. This minimises stress to your pet and lets you relax while away from home.

We are confident with all pets, from cute and cuddly to spiky and slimy.

Vet Visits

Helping in the most difficult of times.

We offer the service of transporting your pet to the vet and can accompany you both, or individually, in the veterinary practice.
